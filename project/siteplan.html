Shop De Chiaro: Site Plan

Site Name: Shop De Chiaro

Chiaro is latin for "light" and its something that is especially needed when creating chiaroscuro effects in art and design. This website is an art portfolio for my black and white art, showcasing the interplay of light and shadow. Aside from that, my instagram account where I share my art is i.am_chiaro so it makes sense as to why this is "Shop De Chiaro".

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase my art portfolio and provide a platform for selling my artwork. It serves as a digital gallery where visitors can explore my creations, learn about my artistic journey, and contact me for inquiries or commissions.

Scenarios

This website will cater to various scenarios, including:

  • Where can I find unique black and white artwork?
  • Can I commission a custom art piece?
  • How do I create chiaroscuro techniques?

Color Schema

The color scheme for this website is inspired by the chiaroscuro technique, emphasizing the contrast between light and dark. The primary colors used are:

  • --primary: hsla(260, 7%, 8%, 1);
  • --secondary: hsla(240, 3%, 22%, 1);
  • --background: hsla(225, 7%, 11%, 1);
  • --cards: hsla(0, 0%, 37%, 1);
  • --accent: white;

Typography: Poltawski Nowy

As of now it is decided to just use one font throughout the website.

Title: font weight: 400, font style: italic;

Headers: font-weight: 500

Text: font-weight: 400
